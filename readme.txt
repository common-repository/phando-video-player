Plugin to easily embed / publish any phando(phando.com) video on website

== Description ==

Phando Video Player plugin enables you to easily embed your videos from your phando account into your wordpress website.

You can search for any phando videos from wordpress admin panel and quickly publish them on your website.
Published videos will have a phando video player linked to their phando account.

= Premium Version Features =

* play ads
* Embed your brand's logo
* Show closed captions/subtitles

[Contact for Premium version](https://corp.phando.com/contact-us/)

== Installation ==

Upload Phando Video Plugin, activate it.
Authorize Plugin, to link your phando account.

You are good to go
